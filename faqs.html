FAQs

Are there gym member initiation fees at Advanced Wellness?

We believe that once you join Structure Health & Fitness, you'll be hooked for life. We're a family-owned fitness business, and you won't experience any hidden fees or up-sells.

Can I cancel my membership at any time?

At Structure Health & Fitness, members receive annual contracts because we want to ensure that we work with you on your fitness goals throughout the year to achieve the results you want. If a member moves out of the area, becomes injured, or is otherwise unable to work out for other reasons, we will certainly be fair and accommodate these situations on a case-by-case basis. If a member decides to just stop coming and wishes to cancel their annual contract, they are free to do that but will pay (retroactively) our higher month-to-month rate for the elapsed term of their annual contract.

Is there a discount on my gym membership if I refer a friend?

We run gym referral promotions year-round, so you are definitely rewarded for introducing your friends to Structure Health & Fitness! Just ask us for details.

Do you run women's fitness sessions?

Yes — we run women's fitness sessions at our gym. For details, please contact us.

What's included in the Structure Health & Fitness nutrition session?

Our nutrition specialists have formulated a broad range of healthy meal options to sustain appropriate energy levels while keeping you healthy and looking fit. You will be asked to keep a food log, which will be analyzed by the nutritionist. Specific recommendations will be provided based on your current food log and your ability to make sustainable changes.
